## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Your words deserve a beautiful home.

Proactive Diary is a private daily writing practice app designed for people who want to build a consistent journaling habit. No cloud sync, no social sharing, no AI reading your thoughts. Just you and the page.

BEAUTIFUL BY DESIGN
Choose from 10 diary colors, 5 paper textures, 4 canvas styles, and 3 layouts. Add your initials as a personal mark. Every detail is crafted to make your writing experience feel intentional and personal.

DAILY WRITING PRACTICE
- Daily writing prompts to spark reflection
- Auto-save so you never lose a thought
- Word count tracking
- Writing streak counter to build consistency
- Mood tracking with every entry

SMART JOURNAL
- Full-text search across all entries
- Calendar heatmap showing your writing days
- Mood trend visualization
- Weekly digest every Sunday
- "On This Day" memories from past entries

GOALS & REMINDERS
- Set up to 3 personal goals (daily, weekly, or monthly)
- Check in daily and track your streak
- Customizable writing reminders
- Smart fallback nudges if you miss your reminder

YEAR IN REVIEW
- Generate a beautiful PDF book of your year's writing
- Export entries as JSON or plain text anytime

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
All your diary entries stay on your device. We never collect, transmit, or read what you write. The only network connection is for subscription management via Google Play.

FREE TO START
Write your first 10 entries free. Then choose a plan that works for you:
- Monthly subscription
- Annual subscription (save 17%)
- Lifetime access (one-time purchase)
